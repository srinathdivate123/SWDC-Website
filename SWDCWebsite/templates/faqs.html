Login

This happens in the following cases:

  • This is the first time you're logging in and you've not changed your default password:
    Let's say your actual PRN is 00000000 but you had entered it as 11111111 during registration. Then your password will be set as 11111111.
    So please contact your Coordinator, come to know the PRN which you entered during registration and enter that as your password. Once you have logged in, you can change your PRN to the actual one in the Profile Section. You can also change your password after login.

  • You have changed your default password to a new password: In this case you can use the Forgot Password option on the login page to set a new password for your account.

    • This may happen if you had registered with an incorrect mail address. Let's say your actual mail ID is test@vit.edu but while registering you had entered it as test123@vit.edu. And now if you're trying to login using test@vit.edu, it will not be possible.

    So we request you to reach out to your Coordinator or Activity Head and get to know your email address that you had entered while registering. You should use this email address to login for the first time. After you have logged in, there is an option available in the Profile section to change your email address to your actual one.
